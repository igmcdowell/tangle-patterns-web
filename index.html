Your Tangles, Everywhere.

Tangle Library for iPhone and iPad lets you bring drawing resources wherever you go. The app includes a filterable list of 40 different tangles and instructions on how to draw them.

You can also use the built in photo features to take pictures of your finished Zentangle® creations and keep them organized against the Tangle Library patterns for easy reference and sharing.


(Available end of January 2014!)

Learn From The Expert

The Tangle Library comes with step-by-step guides on how to draw 40 tangles created by artist, published author, and Certified Zentangle Teacher Sandy Bartholomew. Sandy is well known for her popular Yoga For Your Brain series of books and tangle cards. The artwork for Tangle Library was originally created for the well reviewed Tangle Cards original edition.

New to Zentangles?

All you need to get started is a #2 pencil, a fine-point drawing pen, and some quality drawing paper. Sandy sells a fantastic Zentangle Accessory Kit with everything you need to get going. You can also assemble an ad-hoc kit on the cheap from the internet or an art supply store if you're getting started and only need some of the supplies.

The Zentangle® Method is an easy-to-learn, relaxing, and fun way to create beautiful images by drawing structured patterns. It was created by Rick Roberts and Maria Thomas. "Zentangle" is a registered trademark of Zentangle, Inc. Learn more at zentangle.com.
